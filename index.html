Our Story

Baking with Love Since Day One

At Hassani Bakery, we believe that great bread starts with great ingredients and even greater passion. Located in the heart of Port Harcourt, we've been serving our community with freshly baked goods that bring families together around the table.

From our signature Hassani Marble Bread to our delicious pastries and cakes, every item is crafted with care using traditional techniques and the finest ingredients. We're not just a bakery – we're part of your daily life, your celebrations, and your cherished moments.
